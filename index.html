Our Services

CortexOne is a scientific AI consultancy that helps companies extract, organize, and reason over structured and semi-structured knowledge. Our mission is to bring the full potential of domain-specific AI to your fingertips — responsibly, scalably, and precisely.

  • Codebase Reasoning: Extracting knowledge from source code, APIs, symbols, and internal docs using AST-based parsing and enriched LLM training.
  • Structured Knowledge Extraction: Turning spreadsheets, JSON, logs, or proprietary formats into clean, queryable representations for LLMs.
  • Fine-Tuned LLMs: Training domain-specific models that understand your company’s language, behavior, and architecture.
  • Vector Search & Retrieval Augmentation: Structuring data for powerful in-context retrieval, enabling long-term memory, QA, and reasoning.
  • Multi-Agent Systems: Designing AI agents that cooperate to retrieve, enrich, and compose answers or actions from complex inputs.
  • Self-Hosted Architectures: Building privacy-preserving AI deployments fully under your control — no cloud lock-in.
